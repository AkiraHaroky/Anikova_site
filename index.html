    Hatha Yoga

    the direction of yoga, which since ancient times combines its first stages: the ethical and moral aspects of Yama and Niyama, Asanas, Pranayamas, as well as the cleansing techniques of Shatkarm.

    Ashtanga-Vinyasa Yoga

    a kind of hatha yoga, which is a dynamic practice in which asana sequences pass into one another through vinyasa (sequences of movements accompanied by breathing), bandhi (energy locks), drishti (concentration of the gaze on different areas of the body).

    Iyengar Yoga

    A characteristic feature of this direction is the detailed detailed construction of the body in asanas with various auxiliary materials - props in the form of bricks, belts, rollers, blankets, etc., also a special place is given to safety. Each asana is performed for a long time and the transition to the next asana is slow and smooth.
